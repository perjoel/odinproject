Mashed Potatoes

Home

Description

This mashed potatoes recipe shows you the proper technique for making buttery, fluffy, lump-free mashed potatoes every time for the ultimate comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Whole milk
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Boil potatoes
  3. Drain potatoes
  4. Mash potatoes with milk and butter
  5. Add salt and pepper as needed
  6. Enjoy!
